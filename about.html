About us

Bringing space closer to you

Have you ever looked up at the sky and wondered what life is like in outer space? Well, now you can be closer than ever to your favourite celestial bodies.
Our company was started in 1997 by NASA enginner and entrepreneur Tamas Daria in New York with the mission of making space more accessible to the general public. Our company has been met with positive reviews from critics and customers alike.
